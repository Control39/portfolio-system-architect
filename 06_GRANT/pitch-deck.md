# Pitch Deck: Cognitive Systems Architecture

---

## Slide 1: Problem & Vision

### Problem
> Non-technical professionals cannot enter IT architecture without years of study.
> Current competency assessment is subjective and non-verifiable.

### Vision
> Evidence-based system demonstrating systemic thinking in real-time.
> AI-orchestrated architecture accessible to non-coders.

### Quote
> «Я не пишу код — проектирую системы, где Я управляю ИИ»
