    For HR & Recruiters: Evaluating a Cognitive Architect

    👤 Candidate Profile: Ekaterina Kudelia

    Role: Cognitive Systems Architect Experience: 2+ years of AI-led system architecture Specialization: Transforming non-technical professionals into architects through systematic methodology

    🎯 What Makes This Candidate Unique

    This is not a traditional developer profile. Instead, it represents a new type of professional:

    1. Architect of Systems Thinking - Designs cognitive processes, not just code
    2. AI Orchestrator - Directs AI agents to implement complex systems
    3. Methodology Creator - Built a replicable path from "zero IT" to "enterprise architect"
    4. Production-Ready Mindset - Every component is deployable, monitored, and secured

    DevOps & Cloud Production Kubernetes, GitOps, multi-cloud readiness Expert
    Security & Compliance Financial-grade security implementation Advanced
    Cross-Platform Automation PowerShell, Bash, Python automation Expert

    Soft Skills & Leadership

    Competency Evidence

    Problem-Solving Solved "how to measure systemic thinking" through IT-Compass methodology

    Teaching Ability Methodology designed to help others replicate the journey

    💼 Business Value Proposition

    For Organizations Seeking Digital Transformation

    1. Accelerates Team Development
    2. Methodology can train junior developers 3x faster

    3. Reduces onboarding time for complex systems

    4. Bridges Business & Technology

    5. Understands both technical implementation and business impact

    6. Creates systems that solve real business problems

    7. Future-Proofs Architecture

    8. AI-native approach prepares organization for next-generation development
    9. Production-ready patterns reduce technical debt

    Salary Benchmark

    Based on demonstrated competencies and Russian market rates:

    • Junior Architect: $60,000-$80,000
    • Mid-Level Architect: $80,000-$120,000
    • Senior Architect: $120,000-$180,000
    • Lead Cognitive Architect (this profile): $150,000-$250,000

    Justification: Combines architecture, AI, DevOps, and methodology creation at expert level.

    🏢 Relevance for Different Company Types

    For Russian Enterprises (Yandex, Sberbank, Tinkoff)

    • Yandex Cloud expertise - Directly applicable to Yandex's container services
    • Financial sector compliance - Security implementation meets bank standards
    • Legacy modernization - Shows how to incrementally migrate to microservices

    For IT Integrators (Krok, IBS, Lanit)

    • Prescriptive architectures - Reusable blueprints for client projects
    • Automation expertise - Reduces manual deployment errors
    • Cost optimization - Efficient resource utilization patterns

    For Product Companies

    • Rapid prototyping - 80% faster development through AI assistance
    • Quality assurance - 85%+ test coverage ensures reliability
    • Scalability - Production-ready patterns from day one

    📈 Career Trajectory & Growth Potential

    Short-Term (0-6 months)

    • Lead architecture for 1-2 key systems
    • Establish AI-assisted development practices
    • Mentor junior architects using IT-Compass methodology

    Medium-Term (6-18 months)

    • Director of Architecture or CTO-track position
    • Scale methodology across organization
    • Contribute to industry standards for cognitive architecture

    Long-Term (18+ months)

    • Industry thought leadership
    • Consulting/advisor roles for digital transformation
    • Create educational programs for next-generation architects

    ❓ Interview Questions to Ask

    Technical Validation

    1. "Walk me through your decision to use Kubernetes vs. simpler deployment options"
    2. "How do you ensure security in an AI-assisted development workflow?"
    3. "What metrics would you track to prove the value of your architecture?"

    Methodology & Approach

    1. "How would you adapt your IT-Compass methodology for our team?"
    2. "What's the biggest challenge in transitioning from coder to architect?"
    3. "How do you balance innovation with production stability?"

    Business Impact

    1. "What ROI would you expect from implementing your approach in our organization?"
    2. "How would you measure the success of a cognitive architecture initiative?"
    3. "What risks are associated with AI-assisted development, and how do you mitigate them?"

    📋 Evaluation Checklist for HR

    • [ ] Technical Depth: Verified through portfolio review
    • [ ] Business Alignment: Understands how technology creates value
    • [ ] Cultural Fit: Evidence of persistence, innovation, teaching ability
    • [ ] Growth Potential: Clear trajectory to leadership roles
    • [ ] Market Relevance: Skills match organizational needs
    • [ ] Compensation Alignment: Salary expectations match demonstrated value

    📞 Next Steps

    1. Technical Interview with lead architect to validate depth
    2. Methodology Discussion with team leads to assess fit
    3. Business Case Review with stakeholders to align on value
    4. Reference Check of portfolio and methodology impact
